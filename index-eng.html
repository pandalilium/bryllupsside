There is a wishlist available at Kitchn. There was one at Tilbords too, but as the store went out of buisness the list is now unavailable. We are working og a replacement. Sadly, those are Norwegian stores, so the sites are not available in English.

Having that said, you are more than welcome to not bring a gift as well. We are way more fortunate than most, so your money is just as well spent on those who need it more.
